- **Examples**: Theme preferences, language settings, currency preferences
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device (localStorage)

- **Storage**: Stored locally (IndexedDB), then optionally uploaded to analytics service
- **Retention**: 90 days locally, as per analytics service policy
- **Legal Basis**: Consent (explicit opt-in required)
- **Anonymization**: Wallet addresses are hashed before analytics

## üåê Data Storage and Location

### Client-Side Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device**:
- **localStorage**: User preferences and settings
- **IndexedDB**: Transaction history and analytics data
- **Session Storage**: Temporary session data

### Data Location

- **Primary Storage**: Your device (browser storage)

### Data Transfer

- **Blockchain Transactions**: Transactions are processed on public blockchains (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
- **Public Data**: Transaction hashes and public addresses are visible on blockchain explorers

### Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to:
- Limit how your data is processed
- Pause data collection temporarily

**How to Exercise**: Disable specific features in app settings

### Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to:
- Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time
- Revoke analytics consent

**How to Exercise**: Update consent preferences in app settings
